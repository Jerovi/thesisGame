Quick Facts

  • Starry nights are best viewed in areas with low light pollution.
  • The Milky Way is visible on clear, moonless nights.
  • Van Gogh's "The Starry Night" was painted in 1889.

About Starry Nights

Starry nights have captivated humanity for centuries. The twinkling lights in the sky evoke wonder, mystery, and a sense of infinity. From ancient astronomers to modern stargazers, the night sky inspires art, science, and reflection.

One famous depiction is Vincent van Gogh's "The Starry Night," a swirling masterpiece that captures the dynamic energy of the cosmos. Whether you're gazing at the real thing or admiring it in art, starry nights remind us of the vast universe beyond our world.
